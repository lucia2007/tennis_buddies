About Us

We are a small local tennis club which is welcoming and open to new tennis members. We appreciate that it can often be difficult to find someone to practice or play matches with, so we have developed this app which will hopefully make it easier for you to find your ideal tennis partner, or opponent. You can also book a court up to a week in advance to avoid disappointment.

Find Your Ideal Tennis Buddy

When you register with us and create your profile, you are welcome to search our players' catalouge for an ideal partner/opponent. You can look at their level, preferences, availability and more.

Book a Court

To avoid coming to a busy club with occupied tennis courts, make a reservation beforehand and play when it suits you best.

Get in Touch

Please feel free to get in touch with us, should you have any questions or queries. We will be happy to get to know you and see you on the courts.
